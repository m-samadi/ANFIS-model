- All units are in cm.
- The distances are considered in the range of (0, 100) cm.
- It is assumed that 25 cm is the maximum distance to detect any obstacles in the scenarios.
- Classes:
	Turn-Right-Slightly (1), 45°
	Turn-Right-Sharply (2), 90°
	Turn-Left-Slightly (3), -45°
	Turn-Left-Sharply (4), -90°
	Turn-Back (5), 180°
	Go-Forward (6), 0°
